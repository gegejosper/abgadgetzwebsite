Why ABGadgetz

Affordable gadgets, honest installment terms

ABGadgetz is home of quality and affordable gadgets like smartphones, laptops and accessories — plus CCTV products and installation services, backed by our own installment management system.

We don't just sell smartphones and accessories — we also offer installment plans to customers who don't have the full amount to purchase in cash.

How our installment plan works

A straightforward process handled in-branch by our staff, tracked end-to-end in our own installment system.

1
Choose your item

Pick a smartphone, laptop, accessory or CCTV set at any branch.

2
Down payment

Pay the required down payment; terms vary per branch and item.

3
Sign agreement

Review and sign your installment agreement with branch staff.

4
Monthly collection

Pay monthly at your branch; our system tracks your balance for you.
